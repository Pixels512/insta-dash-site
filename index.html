FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which social media platforms are supported?
Insta Dash Publisher currently supports TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and Facebook Reels. We publish directly to each platform using their official APIs, ensuring full compatibility and compliance.
How does the OAuth connection work?
When you connect a platform, you're redirected to that platform's official login page (TikTok, Google, Meta). You authorize Insta Dash Publisher to post on your behalf. We never see or store your password. You can revoke access at any time from your account settings or directly from the platform.
Can I manage multiple TikTok accounts?
Yes. You can connect up to 5 separate TikTok accounts and manage them all from one dashboard. Each account has its own content queue, posting schedule, and analytics.
What video formats are supported?
We support MP4 and MOV files in vertical (9:16) format, optimized for short-form video platforms. Videos should be between 3 seconds and 60 seconds for best performance. We automatically validate format and duration before publishing.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All API tokens are encrypted at rest and in transit. We use official OAuth 2.0 flows for all platform connections. Your videos are stored on encrypted AWS infrastructure and are only accessed during the publishing process. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. The Starter plan is always free. If you're on Creator Pro, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Your content and analytics data remain available for 30 days after cancellation.
